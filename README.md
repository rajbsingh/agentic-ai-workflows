## Purpose

Agentic AI is about moving from simple chatbots to AI systems that can reason, use tools, follow workflows, ask for approval, and support complex tasks.

This repository explores practical agentic AI patterns.

---

## Workflow Patterns

### 1. Research Agent

Collects information, summarizes findings, identifies gaps, and prepares a structured report.

### 2. Legal Document Review Agent

Reads documents, extracts clauses, flags risks, and prepares a review summary for human legal professionals.

### 3. Customer Support Agent

Classifies customer queries, retrieves knowledge, drafts responses, and escalates complex cases.

### 4. Sales Assistant Agent

Researches prospects, prepares outreach drafts, summarizes meetings, and suggests follow-ups.

### 5. Business Operations Agent

Tracks tasks, summarizes updates, identifies blockers, and prepares management summaries.

---

## Design Principles

- Human approval for high-risk tasks
- Clear role definition for each agent
- Tool access control
- Logging and auditability
- Safe fallback when confidence is low
- Business process alignment
- Data privacy and security
