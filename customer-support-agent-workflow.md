# Customer Support Agent Workflow

This workflow describes a high-level agentic AI pattern for customer support automation.

The purpose of the Customer Support Agent is to help support teams classify customer queries, retrieve relevant knowledge, draft responses, and escalate complex cases to humans.

---

## Workflow Objective

Improve customer support speed and consistency while keeping humans involved for sensitive or complex issues.

The agent should support:

- Query classification
- Intent detection
- Knowledge retrieval
- Response drafting
- Escalation
- Ticket summarization
- Follow-up recommendations

---

## Target Users

This workflow may support:

- Customer support teams
- Service desk teams
- SaaS companies
- Operations teams
- Product support teams
- Internal IT support teams
- Customer success teams

---

## Agent Responsibilities

The Customer Support Agent may perform the following tasks:

1. Read customer query
2. Identify customer intent
3. Classify urgency
4. Retrieve relevant knowledge base content
5. Draft a response
6. Identify missing information
7. Suggest next steps
8. Escalate complex cases
9. Summarize support history
10. Recommend follow-up action

---

## High-Level Workflow

```text
Customer submits query
  ↓
Agent classifies intent and urgency
  ↓
Agent retrieves relevant knowledge
  ↓
Agent drafts response
  ↓
Risk and confidence check
  ↓
Human review if needed
  ↓
Response sent to customer
  ↓
Ticket updated
